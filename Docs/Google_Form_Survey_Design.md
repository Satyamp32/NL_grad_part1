# Spotify Music Discovery Survey: Condensed 90-Second Version

To maximize response rates and eliminate completion fatigue, this condensed version contains exactly **6 key questions + 1 optional contact field**. It is designed as a single-page form (no complex branching logic required).

---

## ⚙️ Form Setup
*   **Form Title:** Spotify Music Discovery & Repetition Survey
*   **Form Description:**  
    *Help us shape the future of music curation! This survey takes less than 90 seconds to complete. Your responses are anonymous and highly valued.*

---

## 📝 Survey Questions

#### Q1: Which Spotify tier do you currently use?
*   **Type:** Multiple Choice (Single Select)
*   **Required:** Yes
*   **Options:**
    *   [ ] Free (Ad-supported)
    *   [ ] Premium (Individual, Duo, Student, or Family plan)

#### Q2: How do you decide what to play most frequently? (Select up to 2)
*   **Type:** Checkboxes (Multi-Select)
*   **Required:** Yes
*   **Options:**
    *   [ ] My own custom-created playlists
    *   [ ] Spotify-generated mixes (Daily Mixes, Release Radar, Discover Weekly)
    *   [ ] The AI DJ feature
    *   [ ] Searching manually for specific tracks/artists
    *   [ ] Mood/genre hubs (e.g. "Focus", "Chill", "Workout")

#### Q3: How often do you feel Spotify's recommendations or mixes repeat the same songs rather than showing you new music?
*   **Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
*   **Labels:** 1 = Never, 5 = Constantly / Daily
*   **Required:** Yes

#### Q4: How often do Spotify's recommendation features (Smart Shuffle, Daily Mixes, Discover Weekly, AI DJ, etc.) help you discover songs you actually add to your library?

*   **Options:**

  * Almost always
  * Often
  * Sometimes
  * Rarely
  * Never

#### Q5: When you want new music, what is your biggest challenge?
